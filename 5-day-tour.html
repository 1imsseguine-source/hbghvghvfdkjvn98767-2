Desert Passage: 5-Day Marrakech to Fes

This 5-day tour from Marrakech to Fes is a customer favorite, offering an epic adventure that connects two imperial cities via the stunning Moroccan desert. It's the perfect package for travelers seeking deep immersion.

Tour Highlights:

  • Cross the spectacular High Atlas Mountains via the Tizi n'Tichka pass.
  • Explore the UNESCO World Heritage site of Ait Benhaddou.
  • Spend an unforgettable night in a Berber desert camp in the Erg Chebbi dunes.
  • Enjoy a full day exploring the Sahara, meeting local nomads and musicians.
  • Travel through the scenic Ziz Valley with its vast palm groves.

Your Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Marrakech to Dades Gorges

We'll pick you up in Marrakech and begin our ascent into the High Atlas Mountains. After visiting Ait Benhaddou, we'll settle in the beautiful Dades Gorges.

Day 2: Dades Gorges to Merzouga

Journey through the Valley of Roses to the Todra Gorges. By afternoon, you'll trade your 4x4 for a camel, trekking into the dunes for your overnight desert camp experience.

Day 3: A Day in the Sahara

Immerse yourself in the desert lifestyle. We'll explore the region, visit Khamlia village for Gnawa music, and share tea with a local nomad family.

Day 4: Merzouga to Midelt

After a Sahara sunrise, we travel north along the lush Ziz Valley. Your destination is Midelt, a quiet town nestled in the cool air of the Atlas Mountains.

Day 5: Midelt to Fes

Our final leg takes us through the cedar forests of Azrou, home to Barbary apes. We'll arrive in the historic city of Fes in the afternoon, where your tour concludes.

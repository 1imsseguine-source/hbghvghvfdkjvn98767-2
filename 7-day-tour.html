The Imperial Explorer: 7-Day Package

This is the perfect choice for your one week in Morocco. Our expertly crafted 7 day trip through the imperial cities combines rich history with the awe-inspiring beauty of the Sahara Desert into one seamless adventure.

Tour Highlights:

  • Explore the ancient, labyrinthine medina of Fes.
  • Ride a camel across the iconic Erg Chebbi dunes at sunset.
  • Sleep under a canopy of stars at a traditional Berber desert camp.
  • Journey through the dramatic Todra Gorges and Dades Valley.
  • Discover the famous kasbah of Ait Benhaddou, a UNESCO site.
  • Experience the vibrant energy of Djemaa el-Fna square in Marrakech.

Your Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Fes

Your adventure begins. We'll greet you at Fes-Saïss airport and escort you to a beautiful Riad in the historic Medina.

Day 2: Fes Cultural Immersion

Dive into the spiritual heart of Morocco with a guided tour of Fes, exploring its famous tanneries, souks, and madrasas.

Day 3: Journey to the Sahara

Travel south across the Atlas Mountains, arriving at the Sahara's edge for a magical sunset camel trek to your desert camp.

Day 4: Gorges and Kasbahs

Wake to a desert sunrise before traveling to the magnificent Todra Gorges and along the "Road of a Thousand Kasbahs."

Day 5: To Ouarzazate, "Hollywood of Morocco"

Explore the film studios of Ouarzazate and visit the iconic Ait Benhaddou, a backdrop for many famous movies.

Day 6: Atlas Mountains to Marrakech

Cross the stunning High Atlas Mountains via the Tizi n'Tichka pass, descending into the vibrant "Red City" of Marrakech.

Day 7: Marrakech Exploration & Departure

Soak in the atmosphere of Djemaa el-Fna square before we transfer you to the airport for your journey home.

The Grand Moroccan: 13-Day Ultimate Tour

For the traveler who wants it all, our 13-day tour from Casablanca is the definitive Moroccan experience. This comprehensive package is a deep dive into the heart of our country, leaving no stone unturned.

Tour Highlights:

  • Visit all four Imperial Cities: Rabat, Meknes, Fes, and Marrakech.
  • Wander the magical blue-washed streets of Chefchaouen.
  • Explore ancient Roman ruins at Volubilis.
  • Experience a multi-day immersion in the Sahara Desert.
  • Relax in the charming coastal breeze of Essaouira.
  • Enjoy a variety of our best Morocco day tours from Marrakech.

Your Day-by-Day Itinerary

This is a summary of your grand tour. Each day is packed with culture, history, and adventure.

  • Day 1: Arrive in Casablanca, visit Hassan II Mosque.
  • Day 2: Explore Rabat, then travel to the blue city of Chefchaouen.
  • Day 3: Discover Chefchaouen, visit Roman ruins, and arrive in Fes.
  • Day 4: Full guided tour of historic Fes.
  • Day 5: Scenic drive across the Atlas Mountains to the Sahara.
  • Day 6: Full day Sahara immersion with 4x4 excursion and camel trek.
  • Day 7: Journey through Todra Gorges to the Dades Valley.
  • Day 8: Travel the Road of a Thousand Kasbahs to Marrakech.
  • Day 9: Guided tour of Marrakech's palaces, tombs, and markets.
  • Day 10: Your choice of a day trip from Marrakech.
  • Day 11: Travel to the charming coastal city of Essaouira.
  • Day 12: Enjoy a final coastal morning before returning to Casablanca.
  • Day 13: Departure from Casablanca.
